## How to Participate

### Prerequisites
1. EVM-compatible wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.)
2. USDT balance on Ethereum mainnet
3. Completed KYC verification (automatic via wallet)

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Visit Auction Page

Navigate to [Liberty Auction](https://libertybitcoin.com/auction)

#### Step 2: Connect Wallet

1. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
2. Select your wallet provider
3. Approve connection
4. Your wallet address will display in the header

#### Step 3: Place Your Bid

1. Enter bid amount in USDT
   - Minimum: **$1,000**
   - Maximum: **$100,000**
2. Use quick-select buttons: $1K / $5K / $10K / MAX
3. Review your bid details
4. Click **"Place Bid"**
5. Confirm transaction in your wallet

#### Step 4: Wait for Auction Results

- Monitor **Oversubscription Metrics** in real-time
- Check **Top Bids Leaderboard** to see your position
- Track **Clearing Price** as it updates

#### Step 5: Claim Tokens

After auction ends (estimated Q1 2026):
1. Visit auction page
2. Click **"Claim Tokens"**
3. Receive allocated LBTY tokens
4. Any excess USDT is automatically refunded

## Lock-up Discounts

### Optional Lock-up Periods

Commit to holding your tokens longer for discounts:

| Lock-up Period | Discount | Vesting Schedule |
|----------------|----------|------------------|
| **None** | 0% | Instant unlock |
| **6 months** | 5% | Unlock on Aug 10, 2026 |
| **12 months** | 10% | Unlock on Feb 10, 2027 |
| **24 months** | 15% | Unlock on Feb 10, 2028 |

**Example with 12-month lock:**
- Bid: $10,000
- Discount: 10%
- Effective price: $9,000
- Extra tokens: +$1,000 worth

### How Lock-ups Work

1. Select lock-up period during bidding
2. Tokens are held in smart contract
3. Earn discount on purchase price
4. Tokens unlock automatically on vesting date
5. No action needed - direct transfer to wallet

## FAQ

**Q: Can I bid multiple times?**  
A: You can update your bid, but only your latest bid counts.

**Q: What if the auction doesn't reach the max price?**  
A: Final price = last bid price. Lower price = more tokens per dollar!

**Q: Can I cancel my bid?**  
A: No, bids are final once submitted. Choose carefully!

**Q: Do I need to be a BTC holder to participate?**  
A: No, but verified BTC holders get priority allocation.

**Q: When can I sell my LBTY tokens?**  
A: Immediately after receiving them (unless you chose a lock-up).

**Q: Is there a whitelist?**  
A: Verified BTC holders get Tier 1 priority. Others: first-come-first-served.
