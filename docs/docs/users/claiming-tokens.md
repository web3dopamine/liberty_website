# Claiming LBTY Tokens

Liberty Bitcoin offers a **1:10 token claim ratio** for Bitcoin holders. For every 1 BTC you own, you can claim 10 LBTY tokens.

## Eligibility

### Requirements
- Minimum **0.003 BTC** balance
- Bitcoin wallet that supports message signing or PSBT
- Liberty wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or any EVM-compatible wallet)

## How to Claim

### Step 1: Check Your Eligibility

1. Visit [Liberty Bitcoin Eligibility Checker](https://libertybitcoin.com/#calculator)
2. Enter your Bitcoin address
3. See your potential LBTY allocation instantly

**Example:**
- You hold: 0.5 BTC
- You'll receive: 5 LBTY tokens (0.5 √ó 10)

### Step 2: Link Your Bitcoin Address

Visit the [Ownership Verification Page](https://libertybitcoin.com/ownership) and choose your method:

#### Option A: Message Signature (Recommended)

1. **Connect your Liberty wallet** (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.)
2. **Enter your Bitcoin address**
3. **Copy the message** displayed:
   ```
   I claim <your-liberty-address> for Bitcoin address <your-btc-address>
   ```
4. **Sign the message** in your Bitcoin wallet
5. **Paste the signature** back into the form
6. **Verify** - Your addresses are now linked!

#### Option B: PSBT Method

1. **Connect your Liberty wallet**
2. **Enter your Bitcoin address**
3. **Generate PSBT** - Creates a self-send transaction
4. **Sign PSBT** in your Bitcoin wallet (Ledger, Trezor, etc.)
5. **Submit signed PSBT**
6. **Verify** - No broadcast needed!

:::tip No Transaction Required
The PSBT method requires signing but NOT broadcasting. Your Bitcoin stays in your wallet!
:::

### Step 3: Claim Your LBTY Tokens

**Launch Date**: February 10, 2026 at 12:00 AM UTC

1. Return to [Liberty Bitcoin](https://libertybitcoin.com)
2. Click **"Claim Now"** button (available after launch)
3. Your LBTY tokens will be sent to your linked Liberty wallet automatically

## Troubleshooting

### "Signature Invalid" Error
- Ensure you copied the EXACT message (including capitalization)
- Check that you signed with the correct Bitcoin address
- Try a different Bitcoin wallet

### "Insufficient Balance" Error  
- You need at least **0.003 BTC**
- Check your balance on [blockchain explorer](https://blockstream.info)

### PSBT Won't Verify
- Make sure PSBT is fully signed (not just finalized)
- Try exporting as base64 format
- Use message signature method instead
